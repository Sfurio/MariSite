Services We Provide

Wet Set (Mascara Look)

Achieve a natural, mascara-like look with our Wet Set service. We will apply individual lash extensions to enhance the length and volume of your natural lashes, providing a beautiful and effortless mascara effect. This service takes approximately 2 hours and is priced at $100.00.

Wet Set Fill

Maintain your Wet Set lashes with a fill session. We will carefully remove any grown-out or loose extensions and replace them with new extensions, ensuring a seamless and full appearance. This fill session takes approximately 1 hour 45 minutes and is priced at $85.00.

Hybrid Full Set

Enhance your lashes with a Hybrid Full Set. We will apply a combination of classic and volume lash extensions, creating a fuller and more dramatic look. This service takes approximately 2 hours 10 minutes and is priced at $110.00.

Hybrid Fill

Maintain your Hybrid lashes with a fill session. We will carefully fill in any gaps in your lash extensions, ensuring a seamless and voluminous appearance. This fill session takes approximately 1 hour 35 minutes and is priced at $90.00.

Volume Full Set

Get a fuller look with our Volume Full Set. We will apply multiple lightweight lash extensions to each of your natural lashes, creating a voluminous and glamorous effect. This service takes approximately 2 hours 30 minutes and is priced at $125.00.

Volume Fill

Maintain your Volume lashes with a fill session. We will add extensions to any areas with grown-out lashes, ensuring a full and voluminous look. This fill session takes approximately 1 hour 45 minutes and is priced at $95.00.

Mega Volume Full Set

For the ultimate lash transformation, try our Mega Volume Full Set. We will apply an advanced technique involving the application of ultra-fine lash extensions to create a bold and dramatic look. This service takes approximately 3 hours 45 minutes and is priced at $140.00.

Mega Volume Fill

Maintain your Mega Volume lashes with a fill session. We will carefully fill in any gaps and add new extensions, ensuring a voluminous and captivating appearance. This fill session takes approximately 2 hours and is priced at $120.00.
